Welcome!

I'm a multidisciplinary artist based in Northern Virginia. My paintings, sculptures, and "beautiful baubles" are blends of mixed materials, chemical reactions, and imagination. I also create functional art, including musical instruments, furniture, and theatrical scenery and props.

I enjoy working with people to create custom pieces that are unique to their tastes and needs. I'm always on the lookout for opportunities to collaborate with other artists and makers.
