It is fair to say that each piece of my artwork begins with the same question:

“What happens if?”

I find the question to be an everlasting font of ideas, new techniques, and no small amount of "oh…that didn't work" movements. It's this question and all of the others that it invites into my brain that keeps me coming back to my studio, day after day.

As an interdisciplinary artist based in Northern Virginia, I explore my internal and external worlds through colors, textures, patterns, and phenomena. My paintings, sculptures, instruments, environments, and crafted objects blend natural and synthetic materials and give form to the answers I discover.
